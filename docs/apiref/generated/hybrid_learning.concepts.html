Description

Handling of concepts: Mapping to model output, enforcement, and analysis.

Sub-modules

analysis

Concept analysis functionality.

concepts

Concept base class, concept types and corresponding sub-classes.

embeddings

Representation of concept embeddings and modification functions.

enforcement

Handles to attach concept output to models and conduct concept enforcement.

kpis

Collection of loss and metric functions and classes.

models

Models and handles to obtain concept embeddings.

visualization

Functions supporting the visualization of analysis results, datasets, and models
