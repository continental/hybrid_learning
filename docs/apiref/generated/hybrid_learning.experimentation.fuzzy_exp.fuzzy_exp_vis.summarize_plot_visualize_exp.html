Concatenation of per image stats gathering, stats summary, and optional visualization of formula body masks. Summary of hybrid_learning.experimentation.fuzzy_exp.fuzzy_exp_eval.gather_exp_stats(), hybrid_learning.experimentation.fuzzy_exp.fuzzy_exp_eval.summarize_exp_stats(), plot_summaries(), and visualize_most_interesting_samples() with option to loop over several formulas. Formula body values are reevaluated.
