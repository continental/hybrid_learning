Description

Models and handles for concept embedding analysis and model extension. This includes handles for

  • Defining concept models

  • Training and evaluating concept models

  • Conducting a series of concept embedding analysis experiments

Sub-modules

analysis

Handles for conducting concept embedding analysis experiments.

Concept base class, concept types, and corresponding sub-classes.

models

Models and handles to define and store concept embeddings.

train_eval

Routines for training and evaluation of pytorch DNNs with callbacks and calibration.
